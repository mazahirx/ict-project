About Us

PMAS stands for Pir Mehr Ali Shah Arid Agriculture University, which is located in Rawalpindi, Pakistan. It is a public research university that focuses on agricultural sciences and offers a range of programs in fields like agriculture, environmental sciences, and related disciplines.
