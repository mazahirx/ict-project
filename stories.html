Startups

The startup community at the University of PMAS (Pir Mehr Ali Shah Arid Agriculture University) plays a vital role in fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and collaboration among students. By encouraging students to think outside the box, the university creates an ecosystem that nurtures the entrepreneurial spirit, helping students transform their ideas into real-world businesses. This community not only provides a platform for budding entrepreneurs to showcase their ideas but also connects them with mentors, industry experts, and potential investors. Through workshops, events, and networking opportunities, students gain the skills, knowledge, and resources needed to succeed in the competitive startup landscape. The presence of such a community at PMAS enhances the overall educational experience, empowering students to become the next generation of leaders and changemakers in the business world.

Leaders

Ayesha Mansha

Ayesha Mansha, the founder of Sahl, is a dynamic entrepreneur known for her visionary leadership and commitment to creating innovative solutions in the digital space. With a strong background in technology and a passion for addressing everyday challenges, Ayesha established Sahl as a platform aimed at simplifying processes for businesses and consumers alike. Her entrepreneurial journey is driven by a desire to leverage technology to create impactful, user-friendly experiences. Ayesha’s ability to identify gaps in the market and her determination to build solutions that cater to the needs of modern users has earned her recognition as a leader in the tech community. Through Sahl, she continues to inspire and empower others, proving that with the right blend of creativity, passion, and technical expertise, one can drive positive change in the world.

Nasir Touqeer

Nasir Touqeer’s journey from a humble background to becoming a successful freelancer is a testament to determination, hard work, and the power of self-belief. Growing up with limited resources, Nasir faced numerous challenges, but his passion for technology and a desire to create a better future pushed him to learn new skills, often through self-study. With no formal guidance, he navigated the digital world, honing his abilities in web development and graphic design. His breakthrough came when he started taking on freelance projects, initially small but gradually building a strong portfolio. Through dedication and consistency, Nasir established himself as a trusted freelancer in the global market, working with clients from around the world. Today, he is a successful entrepreneur, not only thriving in the freelance industry but also inspiring others from similar backgrounds to pursue their dreams and create their own opportunities. Nasir’s story proves that with hard work and the right mindset, anyone can rise above their circumstances and achieve greatness.

About Us

PMAS stands for Pir Mehr Ali Shah Arid Agriculture University, which is located in Rawalpindi, Pakistan. It is a public research university that focuses on agricultural sciences and offers a range of programs in fields like agriculture, environmental sciences, and related disciplines.
