ARID ?

Joining Pir Mehr Ali Shah Arid Agriculture University (PMAS) in Rawalpindi offers a unique and enriching experience for students seeking quality education and personal growth. Known for its strong emphasis on research, innovation, and practical learning, the university provides a dynamic academic environment where students can excel in fields ranging from agriculture and environmental sciences to business and technology. The faculty is highly qualified and dedicated to nurturing the next generation of professionals, while state-of-the-art facilities and a well-equipped campus support a holistic educational experience. The university's strong ties to industry and community projects create numerous opportunities for students to apply their knowledge in real-world scenarios. With a diverse and inclusive campus culture, PMAS Rawalpindi fosters a sense of community and encourages students to develop leadership skills, engage in extracurricular activities, and network with professionals. Whether you aim to contribute to sustainable development, pursue entrepreneurship, or excel in your chosen field, Arid University is a great place to begin your academic journey and build a successful future.

About Us

PMAS stands for Pir Mehr Ali Shah Arid Agriculture University, which is located in Rawalpindi, Pakistan. It is a public research university that focuses on agricultural sciences and offers a range of programs in fields like agriculture, environmental sciences, and related disciplines.
